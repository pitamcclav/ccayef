Our Services

CCAYEF has different services it offers to the Community to promote better livelihoods of people.

Early Childhood Development

We provided access to quality education for underprivileged children pre-pandemic. Our program included affordable education, around the clock care for the children, and transportation to ensure every child has the opportunity to learn and grow in a secure environment.

Teenage Mother Rehabilitation

This service focuses on providing essential counselling, health education, and mental health support towards the teen mothers. We work with local healthcare providers to ensure children and young people have access to the healthcare they need.

Vocational Skills Training

Vocational skills are one of the most important skills a youth can acquire to start up a business or very start earning some money with limited formal education background. CCAYEF offers a range of these skills to the youth so that they can earn a living.

Adolescent Empowerment

Our mentorship and counselling program connects young people with mentors who can provide guidance, support, and inspiration. Mentors help mentees set goals, navigate challenges, and explore opportunities for personal and professional growth. This provides the youth with the potential to maneuver through the changing world with focus.
